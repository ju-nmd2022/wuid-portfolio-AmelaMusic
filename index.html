About me

My name is Amela Music and I am from Nässjö, Sweden. I am a 22 year old new media design student at Jönköping University. On my free time I like to spend time with family and friends. I also like to travel, work out and listen to music.

Why study new media design?

I have always liked the idea of working in media related areas. Designing websites/posters and coding have amongst other things always seemed interesting to me. I applied for the civil engineer program in media technology in Stockholm and studied that for about 1,5 years before deciding to drop out after realising it wasn't for me. I then looked for other media related programs and found the new media design in Jönköping. It had many interesting subjects and no math or physics which was a big relief. It is also close to where I live and seemed to be the perfect option.
